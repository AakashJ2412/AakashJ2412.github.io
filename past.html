TEDxYouth@DPSMIS 2015



The very first TEDxYouth@DPSMIS event was held 5 years ago, on the 27th of December and had featured the theme ‘The Power of Uncertainty’ which left behind an audience ready to embrace uncertainty and to make the best out of life when it is at its most unpredictable. Our inaugural event featured the youth icon, Nawaal Akram, who connects through her wry humor and inspires in her perseverance in addition to other equally dynamic individuals who shared their ideas, which encapsulate innovation, determination, and spirit. These ideas instilled in the audience the very principle of TED which is ‘Ideas Worth Spreading’. Keeping this sentiment in mind, it was decided to establish TEDxYouth@DPSMIS as an annual event.
The Speakers of TEDxYouth@DPSMIS 2015 were:


Abdulrahman Sajid

Abhipray Sahoo

Anu Jossan

Cyril Anand

Nawaal Akram

TEDxYouth@DPSMIS 2016



The second event took place on 30th November, 2016 with the theme ‘Transcend and Include’. Our 2016 event presented Qatar’s very own Hamad Al Amari, one of the most acclaimed comedians in the region as well as a diverse panel of speakers ranging from student to doctor and traveler to style icon, which perfectly epitomizes the theme of inclusivity. Their ideas stressed on the perseverance of the human spirit, a voyage across the world like no other, and a perspective from of girl far ahead of her years. Each idea, radically different from the other, enthralled the audience and served to make the night a memorable one.
The Speakers of TEDxYouth@2016 were:


Denisse Ann El Mundo

Hamad Al Amari

Layla Al Siyabi

Lubaib Gazir

Dr. Mohammed A Verjee

TEDxYouth@DPSMIS 2017



The third edition of TEDxYouth@DPSMIS was held on 2nd November, 2017 and feautured the theme ‘Breaking through Inhibitions’. With speakers including Mr. Khalifa Al Haroon, whose vast digital network initiatives including ‘iLoveQatar.net’ have made him a youth icon and one of Qatar’s most prolific entrepreneurs, the event featured an eclectic group of speakers ranging from the fields of science to deeds of charity. The speakers and their various ideas stressed upon the notion of abandoning one’s inhibitions due fear, ignorance, and insecurity. The night was one of immense and enlightenment and engagement for the audience, who were captivated by the talks.
The Speakers of TEDxYouth@2017 were:


Arvind Rampal

Khalifa Al-Haroon

Maleeha Fatima

Dr. Said Ismail

Salman Al-Khanji

TEDxYouth@DPSMIS 2018

Our team meticulously constructed an event featuring a panel of interdisciplinary speakers whose ideas served to challenge the paradigm of conventional thinking.
